Gopalan International School

Gopalan International School established in 2004 by the Gopalan Group, which has a long standing reputation of sincerity and commitment in the field of Property Development, Architecture and Organic Farming is situated in the heart of the Silicon City of India and the IT Hub. In the midst of all the hustle and bustle of a vibrant IT community. GIS provides a state-of-the-art facility to provide an international ambience of learning.
Our team is backed by rich educational experience of having worked in various prestigious institutes, and these inspiring minds have created an innovative model of instruction for the pre-primary and primary stages.

About Us

Gopalan International School (GIS) is where your child will find his/her first independent, socially interactive scene. It's a place where your children pick up valuable lessons that leave an indelible mark on their future. It is a place where you as parents can confidently send your children and be assured of them being molded into responsible, confident and successful individuals.

GIS is located in Whitefield. We have a selection of boards to offer, which are: ICSE (Indian board), IGCSE (Higher Cambridge department), NIOS, and CIE (Cambridge department).
